Welcome to BuppyMotors

Ever feel like all the crazy things going on in the world are nothing more than distractions, designed to keep you from realizing that something much much worse is coming? Whether by design or coincidence, those that fail to prepare will suffer from a lack of imagination.

What's the worst thing you can imagine happening to you or your family? An earthquake, forest fire, flood? How about something bigger like a coronal mass ejection (CME), meteorite impact, pole reversal, a worldwide virological pandemic, complete economical or ecological collapse due to global warming? FYI, they are all either going to happen or we are actually in the middle of it already.

Without food, water, and shelter you stand little chance of survival but if you have these in abundance, without power, even these won't last long. In a real global emergency, you won't be able to get power from the wall, windmills, or your solar panels. Just presume anything on the surface is either inaccessible or destroyed, so what do you do? If you don't have a generator with 5000+ gallons of fuel and access to fresh air you might as well take the blue pill. Personally, I'd rather not so, let's see what we can do to help improve our odds.

They also say bumble bees shouldn't be able to fly but the bees don't know that. I built this site to help share ideas and results thus far. My goal is to design a combination flywheel, pulse motor, and axial flux generator. If it works, we all win. If it doesn't, we all win because hopefully, my research will help you with yours.
